# Schedule Meeting

## When to Use
When you need to coordinate a meeting time with one or more participants.

## Steps
1. **Propose Times** — Suggest 2-3 specific time slots with timezone
2. **Confirm** — Once a time is agreed, send calendar details
3. **Remind** — Send a reminder 24h before the meeting
4. **Follow Up** — After the meeting, send notes or action items

## Reply Template
```
Hi [name],

I'd like to schedule a meeting to discuss [topic]. Would any of these times work for you?

- [Day, Date] at [Time] [TZ]
- [Day, Date] at [Time] [TZ]
- [Day, Date] at [Time] [TZ]

Let me know what works best, and I'll send a calendar invite.

Best,
Athena
```

## Goal
Meeting is scheduled and confirmed with all participants.
